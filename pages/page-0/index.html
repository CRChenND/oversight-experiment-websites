Why do so many apps ask for your phone number?

Last updated 2 hours ago

In many services, phone numbers are used for account recovery, two-factor authentication (2FA), fraud prevention, and sometimes customer support routing. But not every phone number request is mandatory, and understanding the common reasons can help you choose when to share it.

A simple rule of thumb: if the app offers 2FA, account recovery, or needs to confirm you're a real user, a phone number request is common. If it is for a generic profile completion or trial sign-up, you can often skip it, use email instead, or choose alternatives like authenticator apps.

Tip: Some services treat a phone number as a login identifier. If that's the case, check whether you can change it later and whether you can remove it after setup.
