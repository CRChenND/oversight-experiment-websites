BenefitsCal

BenefitsCal is a portal where Californians can get and manage benefits online. This includes food assistance (CalFresh), cash aid (CalWORKs), and affordable health insurance (Medi-Cal).

Sponsored by CalSAWS, the Department of Social Services (CDSS) and the Department of Health Care Services (DHCS).
